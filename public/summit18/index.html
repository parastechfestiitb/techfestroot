ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

About AI

The world is changing at a rapid pace and so are the machines that we are making. With time, machines have evolved smarter with the help of Artificial Intelligence. It is the simulation of human thinking process by machines, especially computer systems.

Particular applications of AI include expert systems, speech recognition and computer vision. AI has revolutionised the world of business, gaming, healthcare, weather forecasting and industrial automation. It has a series of advantages with the most basic ones being human error reduction and eradication of repetitive jobs along with immaculate accuracy and staggering efficiency.

As always every coin has two sides and AI is not an exception. Demerits include that they are very expensive and need regular human monitoring. On the darker side, there are also speculations about a potential AI takeover.

WHY ATTEND ?

With major Technology companies and Startups seriously embracing AI in recent times, the AI Summit will bring together Corporates, Entrepreneurs and Investors alike, at common platform, to explore how the Artificial Intelligence means business and more.

For Professionals

  • Explore the Business opportunities and challenges presented by Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Get insights about the social, ethical and public policy implications
  • Understand the impact of AI on job creation, unemployment and wages
  • Learn how to strategically prepare for AI and gain a competitive advantage

For Students

  • Speakers will introduce AI, an area which has gained significant attention from researchers and entrepreneurs around the world
  • Opportunity for a networking session with professionals, the best in the field of AI
  • Lay the foundation for your career by intellectual discussions and individual assessments which will surely add to your expertise
  • A complete hands-on workshop giving a complete experience of the capabilities of AI

Workshop


Samsung is going to conduct a workshop which will focus on application of Voice Recognition with the help of Artificial Intelligence. Main contents of the workshop are :

  • A nutshell of Samsung Bixby Voice Assistant with live demo
  • An interactive session on Variable Sentence Length Intent Determination
  • A presentation on Hierarchical Accent Determination and Application in a Large Scale ASR System & a live demonstration on CACTAS as a tool- Collaborative Audio Categorization and Transcription for ASR Systems.
